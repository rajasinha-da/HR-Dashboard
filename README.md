# HR-Dashboard
HR Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview

This HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of employee attrition and workforce trends, helping HR teams identify key factors influencing employee turnover and make data-driven decisions.

Key Features
Total Employees: Displays the total workforce in the organization.
Employee Attrition: Shows the total number of employees who have left the company.
Attrition Rate (%): Calculates the percentage of employees who have left relative to the total workforce.
Average Years at Company: Highlights the average tenure of employees before leaving, helping identify retention patterns.
Attrition by Job Role: Analyzes employee turnover across different job roles to identify positions with the highest attrition.
Attrition Trend: A line chart visualizes attrition over time, enabling users to monitor workforce trends and identify periods of increased employee turnover.
Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Microsoft Excel
Business Value

This dashboard enables HR professionals and management to monitor employee retention, identify high-risk job roles, analyze workforce stability, and develop targeted retention strategies based on historical attrition trends.
